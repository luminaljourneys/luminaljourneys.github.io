# Action Required — Luminal Journeys Admin Notifications

Hi team,

Admin notification emails from Luminal Journeys are landing in your **spam/junk folder**. This is expected for a brand new domain — email providers don't trust new senders until real people tell them to. One action from each of you permanently fixes this for your inbox.

---

## What to do (takes 2 minutes)

### Gmail
1. Open your **Spam** folder
2. Find the email from **Luminal Journeys** `<hello@luminaljourneys.com>`
3. Click **"Report not spam"** at the top
4. Go to your Contacts and add `hello@luminaljourneys.com` — this whitelists the sender permanently

### Outlook / Microsoft 365
1. Open your **Junk** folder
2. Right-click the Luminal Journeys email → **"Not Junk"** → **"Never block sender"**

### Apple Mail
1. Open **Junk** folder
2. Click the email → **"Not Junk"** button at the top of the message

### Other providers
Look for the email in your spam or junk folder and use whatever "Not spam" or "Mark as safe" option your provider offers.

---

## Why this happens

Luminal Journeys launched recently. Email providers automatically distrust new domains until they build a sending reputation — it has nothing to do with the content of the email. Your one action trains your specific email provider to recognize these notifications as legitimate. It will not fix it for anyone else on the team, so everyone needs to do this individually.

---

## Going forward

Once you've done this, all future intake notifications should arrive directly in your inbox. If you ever stop receiving them, check spam first — and repeat the steps above.
